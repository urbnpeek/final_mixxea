Create a premium, modern, dark-themed music SaaS homepage inspired by the visual style of matchmusic.co.

----------------------------------------
GLOBAL STYLE SETTINGS
----------------------------------------

Typography:
- Use a bold geometric sans-serif (Inter / Satoshi style).
- Hero headline: 80px, weight 800, line-height 1.1
- Section headings: 56px–64px, weight 700
- Body text: 18px, color #B5B5B5
- Buttons: 16–18px, medium weight

Colors:
- Background: #000000
- Card background: #121212
- Secondary dark: #181818
- Text primary: #FFFFFF
- Text secondary: #B5B5B5
- Accent gradient (MIXXEA version):
    from #FF5F1F to #FF8A3D
- Glow shadows use soft orange instead of blue.

Spacing:
- Section vertical padding: 160px
- Container max width: 1240px
- Border radius: 24px on major cards
- Soft drop shadows and subtle borders
